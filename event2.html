WHAT IS WHITEWASHING?

WHITEWASHING is a powerful and immersive spoken word and live music concert that explores the intricate layers of identity,culture,love and truth. WHITEWASHING challenges the audience to think critically about societal norms,history and notion of purity. Through evocative performances, the artists will explore topics such as cultural erasure, the complexities of love,societal expectations and personal empowerment. This is more than just a concert. It is a though-provoking artistic experience.
