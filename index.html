About US

Our Mission

Global Art Coterie (GAC) is dedicated to fostering a global community of artists by providing mentorship, securing sponsorship, and building sustainable partnerships. We aim to strengthen the means of implementation and revitalize the Global Partnership for Sustainable Development, aligning with the 17th Sustainable Development Goal.


Our Vission

To be a leading global platform that empowers creatives through robust support systems, enabling them to thrive and contribute to sustainable Development worldwide.
